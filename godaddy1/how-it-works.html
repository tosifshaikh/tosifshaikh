FAQs

How much is the fee to get my groceries delivered?

The fee to get your grocery delivered is $11.50

What about if I'm not at home to receive my groceries?

We can leave your order with the front doorman, apartment office or by your front door.

How much should I tip?

Whatever you think is reasonable.

How can I reschedule my order?

Send us an email with detailed rescheduling time to info@zipcroc.com.

How can I cancel?

You can call or email us to cancel any order you have placed. There is a $10 Cancelation fee.

What if there Is a problem with my order?

Zipcroc is a delivery company we are not involved with picking or bagging your order so it's better to contact the grocery store to get the problem straightened out.
